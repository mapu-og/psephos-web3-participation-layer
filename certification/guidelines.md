Guidelines
Hey there!  Are you ready to get certified?

1. Why get certified?
This certification gives you the opportunity to prove the concepts and skills you learned throughout your time on Alchemy University. You will submit a project that will ultimately serve as a valuable asset in your developer portfolio to showcase, not only newly gained abilities you learned during your time at AU, but also your individual ingenuity and creativity applied to a project that makes it stand out to others.

On top of all of this, building a final project leaves you with a concrete item to showcase your skills/knowledge to potential employers!

2. What is the criteria?
The final project can take a lot of shapes and forms! Ultimately, you will need to demonstrate the your understanding of EVM chains: showing your knowledge of how they work or by creating an application that reads or writes to an EVM chain.

Here are some examples of valid projects:

Building a full-stack web3 application (ie. smart contract with a front-end)
Showing Smart Contract Functionality via Custom Unit Tests
Building a Front-end for an Existing Contract
Presenting Existing Smart Contract Research/Analysis (ie. MEV/Arbitrage)
Building an open source tool for the community of your product/idea
If you have any questions as to what constitutes a valid final project, join us in the AU Discord

5. Teams
Working in groups is highly encouraged! Teams can set you up for success for the simple fact you may be very good at, say, building great smart contracts and your team member is very good at building great front-ends with React… boom 💥, that’s a solid 1-2 punch for final project building right there! Use this same logic to build out a well-balanced team! As always, you have the option to hack solo if that’s more your thing! 🧑‍🎤

Join the Alchemy Builders Discord to find other builders!

6. AU Certification
We look at EVERY project that is submitted! Once your project goes through our internal team's peer review process, you will hear back from us on whether you are officially EVM chain certified.

7. Project Submission
Final project submissions are open on a rolling basis. This means you can submit any time you want! In general, it takes a few weeks for us to be able to review a project and decide certifications. Please be patient with us as we grade all these amazing projects!

If you submitted a project and have not heard back in more than a month, please ping us directly on the Alchemy Builders Discord!

When you're ready to submit, go to the Project Submission tab.
